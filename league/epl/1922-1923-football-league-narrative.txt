The 1922-1923 season was the 35th season of the Football League First Division. It unfolded under the long-established two-points-for-a-win system, with goal ratio used to separate teams level on points, and it stretched across 66 dated sections and 462 league matches. What follows is a chronological account of the campaign from the opening round in late August to the closing weeks in the spring, with the table updated after every date so that the movement of the championship race, the European-less but still intensely competitive upper reaches, and the pressure below can be followed as it developed.
